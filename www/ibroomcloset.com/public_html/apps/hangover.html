Tyson's Hangover Helper!

Tyson's Hangover Helper! for iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch

OH YEAH, you knew it was Comin'! Join Mike and the gang while you record your evening "Doing stupid stuff when you are messed up!" Whether you are in Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Bangkok or anywhere in between, for the first time there is an app to help you remember what went on the night (or day) before. You no longer need to wonder where that lost friend or family member is or how they received that grievous injury...because you'll have a record of it. Lose a finger, well, Tyson's Hangover Helper will be able to record how and where that digit was lost and post it to Facebook.

The Tyson's Hangover Helper! app allows you to:

  • Geo tag your car or starting location so you can find your way back!
  • Create a custom album for each night or event out!
  • Take pictures and add text for each event!
  • Create a map of places visited so you can remember what the Hell you did the
         night before!
  • And of course, you can post to Facebook along the way!
  • Bonus! There might even be some sultry messages hidden throughout the app!

    • So get Tyson's Hangover Helper App today. You never know when you might lose a friend, relative or limb and need to figure out what the Hell happened
