Santa Watch!

Santa Watch! for iPhone, iPad, Android, Blackberry
and Windows Phone 7

Hey, kids! Christmas is coming and so is Santa - right to your home with Santa Watch! 2.0 in November, 2011.

Santa Watch! lets you track Santa as he journeys to your house from the North Pole! Just tell Santa Watch! what time you want Santa to come down the chimney, and this app does the rest! Santa Watch! will show you where Santa is, and where you are, too!

  • Tell Santa what time to be at your house!
  • Create a Christmas Tree with Custom Ornaments using the camera!
  • Watch Santa's progress across the globe on the map!
  • Get notes from Santa all month long!
  • When you hear the jingle bells, off to bed 'cuz Santa's almost here!
  • All artwork in the app is custom, hand-drawn by our own team!

    • Using exclusive Santa Watch! technology, this app knows where Santa is all month long! And it shows where you are so that Santa knows where to bring your presents! So schedule Santa's visit now and watch his travels on your map the day of arrival, and be good!

    Santa Watch! was the recipient of 3rd place for the Best Children's Christmas App in 2010.
