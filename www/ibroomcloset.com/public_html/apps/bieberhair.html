BieberHair

BieberHair for iPhone, iPod Touch and Android

Here, there, everywhere...BieberHair! Whether you love it or hate it, you’re dying to know: what do you look like with BieberHair?! This simple, fun app puts BieberHair on you and your friends, then Facebooks and e-mails it!!

Cruel Dictator... Teen Heartthrob Dictator!

BieberHair! 2.0 for the iPhone and iPod Touch will add the following features:

  • The hair is now applied after you take a photo.
  • The hair can be moved, sized, and rotated.
  • The photo can be resized and cropped.
  • Photos can now be selected from the camera roll or taken with the camera.
  • The Facebook login process has been streamlined.
  • Facebook photo uploads are now done in the background and are faster.
  • Fixes the broken hair overlay caused by IOS 4.3
