SmokeTracker

SmokeTracker for iPhone, Android and Windows Phone 7

Do you use expensive tobacco or even more expensive medical marijuana?

Screenshots:

Do you use expensive tobacco or even more expensive medical marijuana? Each time you take a toke, look at all the smoke you are blowing out! Extra smoke is wasted smoke and wasted dinero? Now for the first time, the hard working people at iBroomCloset have a solution....and we think you are going to like it.

Use the SmokeTracker App. It will help your track people, type of smokeable substance, duration of the toke and play funny sound bites while you hold the smoke in. The iPad version even will show 4 people at a time on the same screen. If you can hold the magic smoke about 30 - 40 seconds and you will blow out hardly any. That means you got every bit and will start saving money immediately!

This App is fully configureable. Add people, choose smokeable substance, choose background picture for App, choose or add funny soundbites to hear during the holding of the smoke and track your stats.

This APP is guaranteed to make you the life of any party as long as are 18 or older.
