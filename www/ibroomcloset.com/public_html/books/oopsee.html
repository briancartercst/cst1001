Oopsee Dazy - Planting a Daisy

Oopsee Dazy Books for iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch

Hello, my name is Oopsee... Oopsee Dazy. I sure do seem to get into some troublesome situations at times. Whether it's slipping and falling or getting all dirty while planting some flowers, all I can say is, Oopsee... Oopsee Dazy!

This new Children's Book App series is written for younger children to help build their confidence with daily challenges they encounter. It is important for children to have a strong self esteem so they can shine in everyday life. Oopsee Dazy enlightens children through her mistakes by redirecting negative energy and turning each situation into a positive growing experience while strengthening self esteem.

Enjoy the added features that come with this Children's Book App:

  • Inspirational positive affirmations
  • Quizzes
  • Every Time you hear the Oopsee... Oopsee Dazy sound it will remind you that its OK to make mistakes and to learn from them
  • And of course... All artwork in the app is custom, hand-drawn by our in-house team!

Come follow Oopsee on her crazy journey in life and learn how she rises to the challenges she encounters! And be sure to tell all your friends and family to join the Oopsee Dazy Book App Club. Because leaning from one's mistakes shouldn't be allowed to be so much FUN!
