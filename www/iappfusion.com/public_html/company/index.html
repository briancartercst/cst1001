About

iAppFusion, LLC. is a Kentucky Limited Liability Corporation headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky. It was started by three entrepreneurial co-workers who realized an incredible group synergy and decided to maximize their talents and skills to implement a solid database foundation upon which they could develop data intensive applications for mobile devices.

iAppFusion creates consumer applications, specializing in database storage and connectivity, as well as providing custom applications to businesses to allow them to access, leverage, and fully utilize their company data from remote locations. The company's unique focus on the the end users' central reference point - THE DATA - which distinguishes iAppFusion as a company on the move.

Cross Platform Development

iAppFusion's focus is on cross-platform application development. This allows the company to reach the most consumers and businesses with valuable solutions. Starting with iPhone and iPad development, the company will also develop for the Blackberry™, Microsoft™ Phone, Android™, and other mobile platforms.
