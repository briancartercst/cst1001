iAppFusion, Inc. Privacy Statement

This privacy statement was created to convey our firm commitment to protecting the personal information that you provide via our Web site. Listed here are our information gathering and dissemination practices for your review and understanding.

Information We Collect From You and How We Use It

iAppFusion collects and stores only the information that you knowingly provide to us via registration forms on this Web site. These forms are presented for your use to order a product or service, request information, or participate in a survey or contest. Additionally, your IP address may be used to gather broad demographic information to help us improve our site's content.

We do not sell, rent, provide or otherwise share your individual information with any third parties. iAppFusion may, however, use information you have disclosed as follows:


- To send you information about our Company.
- To contact you, when necessary (at your request, for example).
- To help diagnose problems with our server.
- To administer our Web site.

This site may contain links to other sites. iAppFusion is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such Web sites.

How We Use Cookies

Our Web server creates a single cookie on your PC to track the length of your visit. Our site uses the same information gathered from other visitors to analyze site traffic so that we may monitor and enhance performance.

Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Contacting Us

If you have questions about our Web site, you may send an e-mail to: webmaster@iAppFusion.com.

If you would like to change information that you have provided us or if you would like to opt-out of future iAppFusion mailings, please send an e-mail to subscriptions@iAppFusion.com.
