Intro

It's all about the APPS! Mobile Device Applications are becoming a very important part of everyday life. And there is no market like this that is growing so fast. According to Wendy Kaufman of NPR, "In 2007, the market was less than $1 Billion, in 2009, it was $6 Billion. In the next couple years, the market is expected to grow to $20 Billion, or, the same amount Americans spend on childrens clothing each year".

iAppFusion was started around ideas for apps. Data apps, reference apps, fun apps, all kinds of apps. Our specialty is in creating applications specializing in database storage and connectivity, as well as providing custom applications to businesses to allow them to access, leverage and fully utilize their company data from remote locations. The company's unique focus is on the the end users' central reference point - THE DATA.

Cross Platform Development

iAppFusion's focus is on cross-platform application development. This will allow the company to reach the most consumers and businesses with valuable solutions. The company has deployed apps for the iPhone/iPod Touch/iPad, Blackberry, Windows Phone 7, and Android Mobile Platforms.

Data Analytics and Reporting

One of the most powerful benefits in using iAppFusion to develop Mobile Apps is our proprietary database storage system, Pondwater™. Pondwater™ gives iAppFusion, and their customers, the ability to collect real-time consumer patterns. Each time a particular app is used, Pondwater™ collects the location (Geo-tag), the amount of time the app was used, the features used and a time/date stamp. This data can be stored and used for reporting and data analytics to provide very insightful marketing information. Pondwater™ can be hosted by iAppFusion or any third party.
