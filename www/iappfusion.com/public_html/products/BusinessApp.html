Business Apps

Providing custom business applications to allow customers or employees to access company centric data from anywhere.

Software applications, or apps, are the nuts and bolts of mobile business communications. Apps are the smartphone programs you use daily to compose e-mail, update your calendar, edit documents and collaborate with colleagues. But how often, do you as a business find yourself wanting to access your own internal data?

We at iAppFusion strongly believe that mobile device platforms like the iPhone, Android, Blackberry and Windows Phone 7 will become the prominent interface for business applications to perform reporting needs. A company's data is the key and users would like to see more of their business data available at their finger-tips. Sales reports, progress reports, financials, it's all about the reporting and the data.

iAppFusion offers over 30 years experience in data mining and analytics and can help your enterprise integrate your mobile applications with your corporate applications and data.

We offer the following services:

- Provide custom, corporate applications for iPhone, Droid, Blackberry or Windows Phone 7 devices

- Cleansing and consolidation of data for lightening quick retrieval on the mobile device

- Creation of powerful reports at the click of a button

- Creation of new web applications to support your existing corporate applications and data
